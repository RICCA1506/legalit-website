DROPBET MVP is a simple but high-impact digital prototype designed to validate the platform’s core idea: users unlock the ability to make football predictions by watching short video ads, instead of spending money upfront. The MVP focuses only on the essential user loop: unlock, predict, compete.
The experience starts with a home screen centered around the weekly football challenge. Users are invited to unlock access to the prediction game through a clear call-to-action. Once they enter the unlock flow, they simulate watching a small number of short ads, shown through a progress bar and simple step-based progression. After the unlock is completed, the user gains access to the weekly matches.
In the prediction section, users can make picks on a selected group of football games. For each match, they choose outcomes such as 1X2, over/under, or a special branded pick that makes the experience more game-like and distinctive from traditional betting interfaces. The goal is not to recreate a full sportsbook, but to create a lightweight competitive prediction experience.
After submitting predictions, the user is directed to a leaderboard-based environment where performance is translated into points and ranking. The leaderboard is a central part of the MVP because it introduces the competitive and social dimension of DROPBET. Users are not just making predictions individually; they are competing for status, visibility, and rewards. Weekly rankings and top-player highlights reinforce this sense of progression and community.
The MVP does not include every future feature of the platform. It intentionally excludes advanced systems such as leagues, social feeds, account customization, or complex monetization logic. Its purpose is to validate the core concept in the fastest and most effective way possible: can users understand the model, engage with the unlock mechanic, enjoy the prediction flow, and feel motivated by the leaderboard dynamic?
In essence, the DROPBET MVP is a focused interactive prototype that demonstrates a new kind of prediction platform: ad-powered instead of loss-powered, competitive instead of purely transactional, and designed around engagement, ranking, and community rather than traditional betting behavior.
